Fun MotherFungi Facts Watch


YEAR

2017-Present

DESCRIPTION

At a farming conference I stumbled upon a workshop that focused on the cultivation of mushrooms. Instantly falling in love with these strange and beautiful specimens I decided to make it my focus during my farming apprenticeships to study mycology and the benefits of fungi within agriculture. Since we are a very mycophobic in America, I wanted to create a series that would dispell some of our anxiety centered around this important and neglected aspect of nature.

Bayard Cutting Arboretum WEBSITE


CLIENTS

New York State Park - Bayard Cutting Arboretum

YEAR

2015 - Present

DESCRIPTION

While interning as a farm hand on the State Park's Community Supported Agriculture farm, I found that I could help the Arboretum in the off season by updating the content of their website. With a focus on education, I added the 'Our Collections' page to highlight certain specimens around the park as well as updated the CSA membership information. While I am no longer planting organic vegetables for the local community, five years later my skills are best utilized by updating the community via their online presence. Additional responsibilities include updating events on Eventbrite, and graphic design of visitor brochures.

Languages - HTML5, CSS, PHP
