Fun MotherFungi Facts Watch


YEAR

2017-Present

DESCRIPTION

At a farming conference I stumbled upon a workshop that focused on the cultivation of mushrooms. Instantly falling in love with these strange and beautiful specimens I decided to make it my focus during my farming apprenticeships to study mycology and the benefits of fungi within agriculture. Since we are a very mycophobic in America, I wanted to create a series that would dispell some of our anxiety centered around this important and neglected aspect of nature.

TOOLS

Adobe Creative Suite (AE, AI, PS)

Pencil & Paper
